💡 Did You Know?

Mental Age is different from your chronological age. It reflects how you think, feel, and perceive the world around you. Some people have a mental age younger than their actual age, meaning they maintain a youthful outlook and adaptability.

Energy Age measures your vitality and physical energy levels based on your lifestyle choices. Regular exercise, social connections, and positive stress management can keep your energy age much younger than your real age!
