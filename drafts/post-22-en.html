Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) üí¨

Q. Can I really create an app with zero coding knowledge?
A. Yes, you can. No-code tools like Glide, Bubble, and Notion allow you to create basic apps without any coding. The idea and planning are 80% of the work - technology is only 20%.
Q. Can apps made by people in their 50s really succeed?
A. Absolutely. Smartphone usage among people in their 50s-60s exceeds 95%. An app created by someone who understands the real problems their age group faces is exactly what they need most.
Q. I have ideas but don't know where to start.
A. First, sketch 3-5 key screens on paper. Put aside complex features for later and focus on "If the user could only do ONE thing, what should it be?" Then follow along with no-code tool tutorials.
